Powerful & Intuitive Software

WizeMice software delivers a blend of user-friendly design and advanced functionality. Its intuitive touchscreen interface streamlines effect navigation and customization, enabling effortless sound creation.

Analog Effect Card

Some tones like analog preamps, overdrives, distortion, vintage fuzz are irreplaceable.

While WizeMice offers high-quality digital effects, it also features hybrid functionality through Analog FX Cards that can have it’s parameters and presets controlled by our pedal via touchscreen and MIDI.
